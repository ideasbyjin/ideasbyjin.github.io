Hugo Migration

Hugo migration update
February 20, 2021 · 1 min
Table of Contents

I’ve recently migrated my blog to Hugo instead of Jekyll. It’s an ongoing process and there might be some broken elements (e.g. images) but will get to updating these soon. In the meantime, enjoy the new format.

I’ll do a post at some stage about how I’ve done this. Long story short, I was a bit tired of Jekyll’s dependency on Ruby which required a lot of tinkering on my Mac (with the Big Sur update). In fact, I’m still not sure how the Ruby version permutations had ended up working out for me, but I decided to go for something more modern and sleek. Not to mention searchability, an archive format, and reading time estimates were huge wins for me.

Enjoy!
